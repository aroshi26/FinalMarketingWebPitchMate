PitchMate Mobile & Web Application

PitchMate is dedicated to simplifying cricket management by offering a centralized digital solution that bridges the gap between players, coaches, and administrators. Our mission is to provide efficient scheduling, performance tracking, medical monitoring, and seamless communication, ensuring that teams operate at peak efficiency both on and off the field.

PitchMate Web Application

The PitchMate Web Application streamlines cricket management for admins, coaches, and medical staff. It offers a dashboard for team insights, player management for tracking stats and fitness, and match & session management for scheduling. Performance analytics provides data-driven insights, while medical reports monitor injuries and recovery. A communication hub ensures seamless messaging, and resource management tracks equipment. Secure role-based access controls user permissions. PitchMate enhances efficiency, performance tracking, and communication in cricket management

PitchMate Mobile App

The PitchMate Mobile Application is designed for players to stay informed and engaged with their team. The Personal Dashboard provides access to match schedules, stats, and training progress. With Performance Tracking, players can view detailed insights into both personal and team performance. The app ensures players never miss a session with Training Session notifications and tracks attendance. The Medical Updates section keeps players informed about injury recovery and medical reports. Coach Feedback & Tips helps players improve with direct insights from coaches. Match Notifications provide instant updates on upcoming games, venues, and timings, while the Team Communication feature allows players to connect seamlessly with coaches and teammates. PitchMate keeps players organized, informed, and performance-driven.

Dashboard

The Dashboard provides an overview of team performance and key statistics, ensuring users can quickly assess the team's standing. It also displays recent matches and upcoming fixtures, keeping everyone updated on schedules. Additionally, real-time notifications and alerts inform users about important events, such as match updates, training sessions, or medical reports. The dashboard serves as a centralized hub, offering quick access to key metrics, helping coaches, players, and admins make informed decisions efficiently.

Performance Stats

The Stats & Analytics tab provides detailed insights into both team and individual performances. Users can access comprehensive player and team statistics, including batting and bowling analytics, to assess performance at various levels. Session-based performance tracking enables users to evaluate improvement over time, while win/loss trends and insights offer a strategic overview of past matches. This section also features data visualization tools, such as graphs and charts, making it easier to interpret performance metrics and adjust strategies accordingly.

Matches

The Matches & Sessions section allows users to manage all aspects of match and training schedules. It includes comprehensive match schedules for different formats such as T20, ODI, and Test, enabling easy planning and tracking. Users can also add new matches and update existing ones with relevant details. Training sessions are seamlessly scheduled within this tab, with options for player attendance tracking to monitor participation. Additionally, this section includes performance analysis for each session, helping coaches assess players' progress and areas that need improvement.

Team Compositions

The Players & Teams section is dedicated to player management and team structuring. Each player profile contains key statistics, past performances, and relevant details. Coaches and admins can manage teams by adding or removing players as needed. To ensure player well-being, this tab also tracks injury and fitness reports, allowing medical staff to update health conditions regularly. The system maintains a player availability status, ensuring that coaches are aware of who is fit for selection. Additionally, the player performance history records detailed analytics on every athlete, helping with data-driven decisions for squad selection and strategy formulation.
